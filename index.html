Our Services

From small maintenance jobs to larger projects, we offer reliable, trade-quality services with clear communication from start to finish.

Exterior House & Hard Surface Cleaning

Comprehensive cleaning of your home’s exterior surfaces to instantly boost curb appeal. Includes house exteriors, driveways, pathways, patios, alfrescos and pool surrounds — removing built-up dirt, grime and mould for a fresh, like-new finish.

Driveway, Pathway & Access Cleaning

High-pressure cleaning for driveways, paths and walkways to remove dirt, stains and slippery buildup. Improves appearance while making access areas safer and easier to maintain.

Deck & Outdoor Living Area Cleaning

Specialised cleaning for timber decks, pool decks and outdoor entertaining areas. Uses gentle soft-washing techniques where required to protect surfaces while restoring their natural look and keeping spaces clean, safe and inviting.
